Those who reveal their most fundamental beliefs—the thoughts that shape their entire lives—risk a great deal. That is why only a few people do so, usually only those who are truly certain of what they believe. Yet it would be important for everyone to thoroughly reflect on their own foundations so that good intentions have a solid basis and can be put into practice.

That is exactly what I did with the series “ZSF1–15” (link below, in german). It offers a valuable opportunity to question one's own foundations. Because if these are wrong, you cannot pursue a good goal – no matter how benevolent or philanthropic your attitude may be.

I am convinced that if many people examine these results, false worldviews will disappear very quickly – and with them much of the suffering and misery that inevitably arises from lies and half-truths.

With the help of the timeline, the most important points can be checked in a comprehensible and traceable way, so that in the end, one can arrive at an unshakeable state of absolute truth on the most important points. A certainty that is supposedly no longer achievable today.

Don't let anything stop you from setting the foundation of your life on the right course! The consequences will affect your entire future—every minute lost is gone forever.
